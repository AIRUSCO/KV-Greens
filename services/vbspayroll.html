VBS Payroll

HMRC-Compliance Payroll

Our payroll engine is fully compliance with UK PAYE, National Insurance, and student loan calculations. We automate all statutory deductions and generate FPS/EPS XMLs for direct, real-time submission to HMRC, ensuring your business is always up to date with the latest regulations and RTI requirements.

Employee Management

Easily add, update, and manage employee payroll details. Assign tax codes, NI categories, and student loan plans to each employee. Our intuitive dashboard gives you full control over your workforce, making payroll changes and compliance effortless.

Automated Pay Runs & Payslips

Create and manage monthly or weekly pay runs with just a few clicks. Attach employees, calculate payroll, and generate payslips in PDF or CSV format. Payslips can be securely emailed to employees, saving time and reducing errors.

Reporting, Security & Integration

Access real-time reporting, download all HMRC protocol and gateway XMLs, and review submission history and audit logs for full traceability. Benefit from secure, role-based access (JWT authentication) for Admins, Payroll Managers, and Viewers. Integrate seamlessly with your business systems via REST API and enjoy a responsive React frontend for all devices. Our solution is end-to-end tested for HMRC SDST evidence and receives ongoing updates for UK payroll legislation.
